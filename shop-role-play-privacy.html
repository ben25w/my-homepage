Privacy Policy – Shop Role Play Register

Shop Role Play Register does not collect, store, transmit or share any personal data. All content added to the app, including item names, photos and settings, is stored locally on the device and never leaves it.

The app does not connect to the internet, does not use analytics, and does not contain advertising. No data is shared with third parties under any circumstances.
